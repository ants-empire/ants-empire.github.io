  1. Open Ants Empire on Roblox.

Why codes stop working

Codes are frequently limited-time. They may expire after events, be single-use per account, or be replaced with new promotions. Always type (or paste) codes exactly as shown. Codes are case-insensitive in most Roblox games, but spacing must be exact.
